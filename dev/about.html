About Us

TensorLy-Quantum is an open-source effort, led primarily at NVIDIA Research, by:

  • Taylor Lee Patti

  • Jean Kossaifi

  • Anima Anandkumar

It is part of the TensorLy project and builds on top of the core TensorLy library to enable building efficient quantum circuit simulation on GPU, using tensor methods.
